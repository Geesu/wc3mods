This document gives the ID numbers for all of the skills which will be used
with the configuration of chameleon.

For example, setting the following in war3ft.cfg:

wc3_cham_random		0
wc3_cham_skill1		14
wc3_cham_skill2		7
wc3_cham_skill3		28
wc3_cham_ultimate	29
wc3_cham_passive	20

Will give you chameleon with the following: 
	Skills: Trueshot Aura, Critical Strike, Shadow Strike
	Ultimate: Vengeance
	Passive Skill: Resistant Skin
