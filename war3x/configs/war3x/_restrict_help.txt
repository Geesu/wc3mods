Below are descriptions of how to use restrictions in war3x. See _help.txt
in /map-specific for information on how to restrict items/races/skills/ultimates
for specific maps only.


Item restrictions (war3x_restrict_items)
=================

a) ankh of reincarnation
b) boots of speed
c) claws of attack
d) cloak of shadows
e) sobi mask
f) amulet of spell shield
g) ring of protection +5
h) tome of experience


EXAMPLE: war3x_restrict_items "ae" would restrict ankh and sobi mask



Race restrictions (war3x_restrict_races)
=================

a) undead scourge
b) human alliance
c) orcish horde
d) night elf sentinels


EXAMPLE: war3x_restrict_races "a" would restrict undead
